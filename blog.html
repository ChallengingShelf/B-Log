First of all thanks for reading, I hope you enjoy my writing. This is just a place for a lot of thoughts and opinions, plus stories from the cybersecurity world.

Daniel Hamilton

In 2026, every company is now a tech company, whether they like it or not. This puts securing these organisations at the forefront of the list of priorities. At least it is supposed to. In 2026 many businesses still do not prioritise their cybersecurity and we see many simple attacks that could are preventable.

Hackers are more innovative and resourceful by the day. The rise of nation state threat actors / advanced persistent threats (APTs) is shaping not only private sector organisations but also the general geopolitical landscape more than ever before. This combined with artificial intelligence lowering the technical entry barrier and increasing complexity / capability and simple volume of attacks means that defense and investment in security is no longer optional but necessary.

There has never been a more exciting time to break into the cybersecurity industry.
